MLOps Foundations

MLOps is the discipline of turning notebook experiments into reliable, observable, continuously-improving production systems. Think of it as DevOps with extra failure modes — your code, data, and model are all moving parts.

Mental model

Three pipelines, not one: the data pipeline produces features, the training pipeline produces models, the inference pipeline serves predictions. Each needs versioning, tests, and monitoring independently.

Practitioner checklist

Can you retrain your model from scratch today? Can you tell which version of which feature pipeline produced your current production predictions? If either answer is "not sure," you have MLOps work to do.
