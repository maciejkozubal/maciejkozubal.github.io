Python

The lingua franca of data science. You won't escape it — learn it well enough that it disappears and you can focus on the modeling and engineering problems in front of you.

Why it matters

Virtually every DS/ML team uses Python. Even if serving is Go or Rust, experimentation, data munging, and model training will land in Python. Invest in fluency early.
